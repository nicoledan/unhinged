Folding Instructions

  1. Fold the paper vertically along the orange dotted line. The hater/lover print on the back should be inside.
  2. Make another lengthwise fold so that the front cover is on top.
  3. Now open the last lengthwise fold and fold it in half horizontally.
  4. Unfold the last horizontal fold. Fold pages 7 and 6 underneath and the front and back underneath. Now you see 4 5 and 3 2.
  5. Fold it in half. Now you should see the front and back cover on one side and 7 and 6 on the other.
